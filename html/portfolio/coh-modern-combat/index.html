Company of Heroes: Modern Combat

CEO 2009-2012

An ambitious total-conversion modification for Relic’s award-winning Company of Heroes® franchise, Modern Combat transports players into the midst of a high-tech war between the current-day Chinese and United States armed forces. Incorporating all-new factions, units, doctrines, gamemodes, artwork, music and maps, we aim to craft the most immersive and enjoyable strategic representation of 21st-century combat possible on the PC.

  • Awarded 2nd best upcoming modification (2011) by the public community at ModDB
  • Worked with a changing development team with a total of 33 members
  • Released and downloaded over 500,000 times
