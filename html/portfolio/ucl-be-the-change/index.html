UCL Be The Change

Co-Founder 2015-2016

Co-Founded the student-led Quality Improvement Initiative at UCL, consisting of medical and pharmacy students. Focused on integrating allied health care professionals to work together to improve patient care and make the NHS more efficient.

  • Recruited a team of 36 UCL Medical and Pharmacology students, managed 5 teams through the communication platform Slack
  • Contacted hospitals affiliated with UCL to find areas that needed improving through departmental audits as our clinical placements
  • Organized and led 5 key quality improvement projects following PDSA cycles and fishbone models
  • Presented and achieved 1st place in the 2016 National NHS Quality Improvement Championship at the BMA house
